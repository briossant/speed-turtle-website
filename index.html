What is it?

"Piquette" is a video game of exploration and adventure. In one of your deepest dreams you find yourself in a room accompanied by Piquette, a small turtle who will accompany you in each level of the game. Players will have to succeed in moving from room to room if they hope to wake up. Between puzzles, fights, courses and many others, will players manage to come out unscathed?

Players will go from level to level in the eyes of the character they play as. The game will be accessible in solo and duo, having for characters: Piketty, the only character accessible in solo, and Piquette, character accessible in duo. Several modes will be available. First of all, when you launch the game for the first time, you have access to the "normal" and "hard" modes. In the first mode, if the player dies in a level, he starts again at the beginning of the level, while in the "hard" mode, the player starts his progression from scratch. Once the game is finished, another mode is unlocked, "level choice", which allows to do any room separately. At the end of each game the statistics will be displayed: the time taken and the number of times the player died.

The levels

The Chase

A big rock just start rolling down the hill and the players must run away while avoiding obstacles to not be crushed by the rock.

The Infiltration

Here the players must dodge lasers and jump acrosse platforms without getting hit, like the spy in the impossible mission movie

The Colosseum

Here, the two players find themselves in the middle of a Roman coliseum in which they will have to survive several waves of enemies, being more and more numerous.

The Plane

In this level, the players balances on a plane and has to control it to avoid a crash by moving on the wing of the plane. However, if the players decide to play in pairs, they must be synchronized to move the plane.







Who are we?

We are all in the first year of the preparatory cycle of Epita and come from a general terminal plus scientific access. We have the ambition to create a complete and original game by realizing it, on the maximum of aspect, with our own hands. This will allow us to acquire the necessary technical knowledge. We are determined to carry out this project which is close to our hearts. Our group is called "Flipped Turtles".

The project we present below is built on the inspirations we had during discussions within our group. These inspirations are based on personal opinions, experiences from playing some similar games and others. We were inspired by some existing games to bring our project to life; we can mention Valve's Portal and Half-Life for the journey and adventure side or Total Mayhem Games' We Were Here for the puzzles and cooperation. There are also other inspirations like the movie Alice in Wonderland or the urban legend of Backrooms.
